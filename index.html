About me


I am a Swedish, 23 year old, junior full-stack web developer that lives in Zurich, Switzerland. I am currently attending a course based on real-life projects with focus on building web applications with advanced technologies to become a Full-Stack Web Developer and I will graduate with a certificate in Web development in mid-April. This course allowed me to attain a strong foundation of technical skills. In addition to my Web Development skills, I also possess a great eye for design. Building state-of-the-art, easy to use, user-friendly websites and applications is truly a passion of mine. Beside my knowledge base, I actively seek out new technologies and stay up-to-date on industry trends and advancements.
